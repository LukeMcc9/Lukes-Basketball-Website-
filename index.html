Luke's Basketball Website

I'm an attorney based in Washington, D.C. I like basketball and rules.

This site is a public notebook of some of my basketball thoughts. Many of my thoughts focus on the rules of team building in the NBA, with an eye toward how teams can potentially exploit gaps in rulemaking.

Not every thought is fully formed. All are open to pushback. I'm sharing publicly in the hope it encourages that.
